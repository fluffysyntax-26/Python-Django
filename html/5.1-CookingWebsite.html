Garlic Pasta Recipe!!!


Garlic Butter Pasta is a simple, comforting dish made with just a few ingredients—pasta, butter, and sautéed garlic. It's rich, flavorful, and perfect for a quick meal. Optional toppings like Parmesan or chili flakes add an extra kick!

Ingredients Needed:

  • 200g (about 7 oz) spaghetti or any pasta you like
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3–4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Optional: chili flakes, grated Parmesan, parsley

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta: Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve about ½ cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. Make the garlic butter sauce: In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter. Add chopped garlic and sauté for about 1-2 minutes, until fragrant and just golden (don’t burn it!).
  3. Combine Pasta and Sauce: Add the drained pasta to the skillet. Toss to coat with the garlic butter. If it seems dry, add a splash of reserved pasta water until it’s glossy.
  4. Season and serve: Season with salt, pepper, and optional chili flakes. Serve hot, topped with Parmesan and chopped parsley if you like.
